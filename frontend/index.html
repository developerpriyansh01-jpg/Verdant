Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI Scanner?
The AI Model is trained on the high-quality PlantVillage dataset containing over 54,000 leaf images, achieving over 96% classification accuracy.
What plants are supported?
Currently, we support common garden plants including Tomato, Rose, Pepper, Potato, Apple, Grape, Aloe Vera, and Tulsi.
Can I use the app offline?
You can access the home page, plant library, and scan history offline. The scanner features a local demo fallback.
