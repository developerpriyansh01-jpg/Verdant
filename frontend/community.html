🌦️ Season Care Guide

Get customized schedules and biological guidelines for all seasonal transitions.

Summer Care

  • Water plants in morning or evening.
  • Protect sensitive plants from direct midday heat.
  • Keep soil moisture levels maintained with mulches.
  • Provide shading screens when UV indexes peak.

Rainy Season

  • Avoid excess manual watering.
  • Clear drain holes to prevent waterlogging.
  • Monitor leaves weekly for molds and fungi.
  • Ensure clean foliage to maximize absorption.

Winter Care

  • Reduce overall watering frequency.
  • Position pots to catch maximum morning light.
  • Shield delicate species from frost drafts.
  • Hold off on major fertilizer application.

Spring Care

  • Ideal timing for repotting/planting.
  • Mix in fresh compost top dressings.
  • Prune away yellowed winter leaves.
  • Nurture emerging new growths.

💡 Seasonal Tip

Always check soil moisture before watering your plant.
